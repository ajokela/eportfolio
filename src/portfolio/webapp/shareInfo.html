Sharing Information
Can Portfolios be shared with non-OSPI users?
Yes, Portfolio folders may be shared with current OSPI users as well as individuals outside of the OSPI Community to whom you grant access.  When creating a folder enter the email address of the individual(s) in the Email Address (Non - OSPI) field.

Can individuals given viewing privileges to a folder see ALL of the information in my Portfolio?
No, you can choose to share any or all of the information you have in your Portfolio with any individual.  The individuals you have chosen to share information with will only see the information you have chosen to share with them.  

How will individuals [given viewing privileges] know that I have shared a folder with them?
We have added a new feature to Portfolio, now when you create a new folder to share, an email message will be generated for ALL of the individuals with whom you chose to share the information.  The email message will provide the individual with directions on where to access and how to login to Portfolio.

How will Non - OSPI individuals access folders to which they have been given viewing privileges?
A unique password will be generated for each Non - OSPI user with whom you chose to share a Portfolio folder.  The email message will provide the individual with directions on where to access and how to login to Portfolio using his or her email address and password.

How secure is Portfolio?
An advantage of Portfolio is the high level of security. The secure server uses SSL (secure socket layer); this ensures that all of your Portfolio information is encrypted and cannot be read as the information travels over the Internet.  For more details about security, read the Privacy Statement.
